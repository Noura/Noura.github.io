In Technologies of Representation, we surveyed how different technologies represent the world in different ways, the tradeoffs to different approaches, and ethical considerations of different approaches. For the final project, student teams did informal audits of generative AI tools to critically investigate how these generative AI tools represent the world, and what biases generative AI might display.

Students projects:

  • Demographic Bias in ChatGPT-4 Age Recognition - Naomi Belay, Charlie Rivers, Brayden Smith
  • Gender Representation in OpenAI Sora - Krish Shajpaul, Jayson Ndungu
  • Demographic Representation in Google Gemini Sports Activities - Reagan Maxwell, Sarah Wolf
  • Searching for Gender Bias in Google Gemini - Scott Rivenbark, Aubrey Poole, Christina Sweeney
  • Gender Bias in Google Gemini Representation of French Healthcare Professions - Hope Hallett, Gabby Geringer, Claire Kelly
  • Moral Dilemmas in Microsoft Copilot - Jimmy Paris, Matthew Sabella
  • Data Analysis with Google Gemini - AC Davis, Corbin Greene
  • Analyzing Political Censorship in DeepSeek - Kieran Kelly, Vincent Soro
