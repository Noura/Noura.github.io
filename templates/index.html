Noura Howell

As a PhD student at the Berkeley School of Information, I research emotional biosensing in everyday life and explore critical alternatives through physical data representations or enactments. Broadening the design space around biosensing, I probe what relationships with physiological data might mean for our social relationships and sense of self. I am advised by Kimiko Ryokai and a member of the BioSENSE lab.
