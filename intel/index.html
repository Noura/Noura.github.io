Connect Anything with Intel's Galileo

ConnectAnyThing (release repo, development repo) makes it possible to use Intel's Galileo microcontroller without writing a line of code. It enables creative play and rapid prototyping. I worked on the client and server side code.

MakerNode (repo), a work in progress, will provide a simple workflow for node.js projects on Galileo.
