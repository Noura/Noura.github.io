Tea Love

UIST Student Innovation Contest Project Proposal

I used to make tea for my younger brother often. But now that he lives in Philadelphia and I live in San Francisco, all I can do is imagine him trudging home through the snow and shivering at his desk, studying. Nagging him to make tea for himself is not the same. Instead, sensors installed by his desk could alert me via SMS that he is studying. In response, I would trigger a tea-making-machine and compose a note. He would enjoy my nag-free message and tea steeped in love.
